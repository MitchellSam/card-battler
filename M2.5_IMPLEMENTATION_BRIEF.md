## 2. Game-end semantics (replaces both old deckOutTrigger paths)

The game ends ONLY at a draw phase: when the turn player must draw and the deck is empty, the game ends (cards already drawn that phase stay in hand; proceed to showdown). Precisely:
- Draw phase draws `drawPerTurn` cards one at a time; if the deck empties mid-draw, keep what was drawn and end the game at that point.
- Mill, Poly, and effect draws NEVER end the game mid-turn. An effect draw (4-flip, Joker) from an empty deck draws nothing (partial where possible: Joker with 1 card left draws 1).
- Mid-Poly deck-out: forced STAND on the current total; the match resumes and ends at the next failed draw phase. (Replaces RULES-GAP #10's fizzle-if-nothing-dealt path — with §5 below there is always a first card, so forced-stand is the only case.)
- A player whose deck is empty simply plays on until their next draw phase arrives.

## 3. First turn: no Battle Phase (new)

`RulesConfig.firstTurnBattle: boolean` (default false). When false, the first player's turn 1 has no Battle Phase — `nextPhase` from main1 goes directly to main2 (or keep the phase but `legalActions` returns no declareAttack; choose whichever is cleaner, but the phase-skip is preferred so the event log reads correctly). Skip-first-draw remains as-is. YGO convention.

## 4. Ace as spell fuel (upgrades RULES-GAP #1)

When an Ace is discarded for Q (buff) or K (debuff), the CASTER chooses 1 or 11 at cast time. Extend the castSpell action's `discardHandIndex` path with `aceValue?: 1 | 11` (required iff the discard is an Ace; illegal otherwise). ⚠ Interaction (ratified with eyes open): with §6 below, K+Ace(11) destroys any monster in the game.

## 5. Polymerization: target is the first card (replaces polyInitialDeal)

Remove `polyInitialDeal`. Poly's blackjack total STARTS at the target monster's card value: number = face value; face card (possible only via Recast typeOverride) = 10; Ace target → caster chooses 1 or 11 when Poly begins (reuse the polyAce pending). The target's card is NOT drawn or milled — it contributes value only and remains the monster being fused. Then hit/stand as normal from the deck.

## 6. No debuff floor (replaces RULES-GAP #5)

A monster whose effective power is ≤ 0 is destroyed (to graveyard) immediately upon the change. Applies to K debuffs and any future effect (Siege Engine). Destruction by debuff is NOT combat: no bank trigger, no wall-punish. Emit MonsterDestroyed with a cause field.

## 7. Joker drawn during Poly: shuffle back + redraw (replaces polyJoker / RULES-GAP #2)

A Joker drawn as a Poly hit is shuffled back into the deck (seeded RNG) and the hit is redrawn. Edge (provisional): if the deck contains no non-Joker cards, the hit fails and the caster is forced to STAND. Remove the `polyJoker` config knob.

## 8. Direct-attack interception (replaces RULES-GAP #8)

If a monster appears on the defender's field while a direct attack is on the stack, the attack does not fizzle — it retargets to that monster ("interception"). If the interceptor is face-down, normal flip-then-combat applies. Edge (provisional): if MULTIPLE monsters appeared, the DEFENDER chooses the interceptor (new Pending). RULES-GAP #7 stands unchanged: if the ATTACKER is removed/bounced/turned, the attack is interrupted and spent.

## 9. Mirror combat generalized (extends mirrorCombat, replaces RULES-GAP #9)

Identical rank AND suit combat (possible across the two decks) = both monsters destroyed, regardless of the defender's position, with NO bank trigger and NO wall-punish (a tie is neither a win nor a loss). This replaces the old wall-mirror no-op.

## 10. Partial-bank poker hands (replaces RULES-GAPS #3, upholds #4)

Partial banks (<5 cards) are evaluated with standard poker categories over the cards available: pairs, two pair (4 cards), trips, quads are all valid; straights/flushes/full houses require 5 and are impossible. Comparison: category first, then standard kickers, then extended kickers where an extra card beats no card (unchanged). Consequence: a 2-card KK pair BEATS a 4-card ace-high. Update poker.ts + property tests (reference implementation must agree on partial hands).

## 11. Ante (new mechanic — Run-mode pacing baseline; sim before fixing N)

`RulesConfig.ante: number` (default 0 = Constructed canonical). At game start, after mulligans, each player moves `ante` random cards (seeded) from the top of their shuffled deck directly to their PUBLIC bank. Ante'd Jokers: redraw-style — shuffle back, ante a different card (Jokers are never bankable). Emit `CardBanked` with cause 'ante'. Design intent: shorter Run duels + live poker layer from turn 1; Constructed stays ante 0.
