MY GAME — RULES!!!
(the official house rulebook. do NOT lose this.)
  1. Deck is 52 cards + 2 JOKERS. Run out of cards → the game ENDS (not a loss). Best 5-card poker hand in your BANK wins!!
  2. Number cards (A, 2–10) = MONSTERS. Power = the number. Ace = 1 or 11, you pick.
  3. 1 normal summon per turn. Monsters 5–7 cost 1 sacrifice, 8–10 cost 2. Summon face-up attack OR set face-down.
  4. SET MONSTERS go sideways (rotated 90°). Set spells/traps stay upright (face-down). Face-up defense = face shows, sideways.
  5. Face cards (J Q K) = SPELLS: play for the RANK effect OR the SUIT effect — never both, chosen on the stack.
  6. ANY card in hand can be BANKED. Banks are public & unlimited.
  1. Stack resolves TOP-DOWN (last played resolves first). Priority → respond or pass.
  2. Combat: bigger power wins (ties ♠>♥>♣>♦). Win attacker-vs-attacker or hit an empty board → bank a card from hand OR yank one from their bank.
  3. POLY (♦) = point at ONE monster, blackjack w/ your own deck. STAND = power ⌈total÷2⌉. BUST = torn up.
  4. Hand limit 6 — discard down at end of turn. Draw 1 each turn (Jokers draw 2).
  5. FLIP EFFECTS: a set monster flipped face-up (or attacked) triggers its number's flip effect.
  6. Punch the wall when you lose = get a free re-draw ↳ NO TAKE-BACKS!!
