# CANON — attach to every design session (House Rules, working title)

**Standing instruction: never invent rules, card effects, shop items, stats, or mechanics.** All game content must come from this document. If a design needs content this document doesn't provide, render a placeholder visibly labeled "TBD" — do not fill the gap creatively. Visual style, layout, flavor text, and character names (Riley, Sam) are yours to invent; game mechanics are not.

## The rules (the only true rules)

1. Each player: 52 cards + 2 Jokers. When someone runs out of cards the game ENDS — best 5-card poker hand in your BANK wins. Running out is not losing.
2. Number cards (A, 2-9, 10) = monsters. Power = the number. Ace = 1 or 11 (player picks).
3. Summoning: 1 normal summon per turn. Monsters 5-7 cost 1 sacrifice; 8-10 cost 2. Summon face-up attack (upright) OR set face-down (sideways).
4. Set MONSTERS are rotated 90°. Set SPELLS/TRAPS are face-down but NOT rotated. Face-up defense = face visible, rotated 90°.
5. Face cards (J/Q/K) are spells: play for the RANK effect or the SUIT effect — chosen when it hits the stack, never both.
6. The stack resolves top-down (last played resolves first). Priority: respond or pass.
7. Combat: bigger power wins; ties broken ♠>♥>♣>♦. Winning attacker vs attacker (or hitting an empty board) = winner banks a card from hand OR removes a card from opponent's bank. Losing to a defender = attacker's monster destroyed AND loses a banked card.
8. ANY card in hand can be banked. Banks are PUBLIC and UNLIMITED (no cap, no bank slots — an item that adds "bank slots" cannot exist).
9. Polymerization (♦ suit effect): targets ONE of your field monsters. Blackjack vs your own deck: HIT draws from your deck; Ace drawn = player picks 1 or 11 at that moment; BUST (>21) destroys the monster; STAND = new power ⌈total÷2⌉.
10. Hand limit 6 (discard down at end of turn). Draw 1 per turn. Jokers = draw 2 cards.
11. Flip effects: a set monster flipped face-up (manually or by being attacked) triggers its number's flip effect.

## Run mode structures

- Deck is always the same 54 cards. It can SHRINK during a run (never grow). Removal floor: 40.
- STICKERS cover a card's effect (permanent for the run). SHEET MODS change global rules on the Cheat Sheet (rarest; whiteout = a rule deleted). SLEEVES are post-v1 — pencil-sketch only, labeled "v2 idea."
- Type conversion (rare "Recast" sticker): number card → SPELL; face card → MONSTER (power 10, 2-sac cost). Never the other direction. Recast is RARE — never a cheap shop single.
- Shop: two pack tiers only — CORNER STORE pack (cheap, common-weighted) and TRADE BINDER pack (expensive, guaranteed rare) — plus a small utility singles shelf. Approved utility item: "Trim a Card" (remove 1 card from deck). No bank-slot items, no Recast singles.
- Currency: in-run money label is TBD (do not name it; render as a coin count with a TBD note). "Allowance" is the between-run starting-money stat only.

## Canonical sticker names (use ONLY these; text must match)

Commons: Peek (look at top 3 of your deck, rearrange) · Scavenge (return 1 random card from your graveyard to hand) · Feint (this monster may switch position once, immediately) · Needle (opponent reveals hand; you pick 1 they discard) · Skim (mill 1 from your own deck, draw 1) · Warning Shot (force 1 enemy face-down monster face-up, no flip effect) · Stall (opponent's next normal summon this turn is skipped) · Rally (all your OTHER face-up attack monsters +1 power until end of turn) · Undertow (destroy this; special summon from your graveyard face-down) · Short Fuse (mill 3 from opponent's deck; destroy this monster) · Copycat (copy a flip effect from either graveyard) · Doorstop (this monster can't be destroyed by combat this turn) · Tax (opponent discards or skips next spell/trap this turn) · Graft (banked, counts +1 rank for tie-breaks only)

Uncommons: Executioner's Toll · Overwhelm · Leverage · Siege Engine · Turncoat · Riposte · Vigil · Second Wind · Grave Robber · Sunder · Landslide

Rares: Recast (type conversion) · Loaded Deck (face-down, counts as 10 in combat only) · Second Chance (first destruction each game prevented) · Wildcard Bank (banked: any suit, for flushes only) · Echo (flip effect triggers twice)
